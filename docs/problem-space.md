# Problem Space

Distributed systems and heterogeneous ledger environments often depend on trust-heavy coordination mechanisms.

In many cases, cross-system interoperability relies on one or more of the following:

- intermediaries
- bridge-like architectures
- oracle-based validation
- gateway coordination
- wrapped representations

These patterns can introduce additional trust assumptions, coordination dependencies, and integrity risks.

This research direction explores whether integrity-related properties of settlement and ledger state can be examined through cryptographic verification approaches across heterogeneous environments.

The focus is conceptual and exploratory.
